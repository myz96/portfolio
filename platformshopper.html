Grocery Delivery Startup

Valuing shopper sentiment and supply availability to guide platform growth strategy

Type
Project Deliverable
Year
2022
Languages
SQL: Snowflake
Python: Pandas
Accenture Strategy was contracted to deliver valuations of shopper sentiment and supply availability and determine their impact on long-run customer revenues. I led the sentiment modelling that determined the effect of wages and ease-of-work for shoppers on the platform on their performance, engagement and utility.

Problem Space

Shopper sentiment has shown a steady decline after the initial large inflow from COVID. Shopper sentiment has become a key area of focus for the senior executive team and is believed to have large performance and on-going brand impacts.

There are two major problem themes identified in the current experience:
  1. Customers are waiting longer to receive their orders as shoppers become more selective about the orders they choose to deliver
  2. If more shoppers are incentivised to deliver orders, shopper sentiment decreases as their ability to take orders diminishes

Proposed Solution

The proposed solution is composed of three key steps:
  • Model the business benefits associated with decreased customer wait times, including incremental customers gained, increased engagement with the platform and higher customer spend
  • Value the long-run effects of decreased shopper sentiment from increased shopper competition
  • Determine the optimal balance between these two countervailing effects to find the steady-state needed for long-term platform growth
