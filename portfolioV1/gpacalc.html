Unimelb GPA Calculator

Enabling Unimelb student to easily and reliably access their GPA scores

Type
Side-project
Year
2021
Languages
Javascript: ReactJS
Python: Pytesseract, Pandas, CV2
Figma
After hearing a recurring need from friends and anonymous forum posts at the University of Melbourne to convert their Weighted Average Marks (WAMs) to GPA equivalents, a friend and I decided to design and build a web app that automatically parsed, scraped and converted University of Melbourne transcripts into GPA equivalents. This allowed for a user-friendly way to easily obtain GPA marks for medical school and overseas graduate programme applications.

Problem Space

Existing online GPA calculators require a cumbersome process of individually inputting transcript information, leaving room for user error and consuming valuable user time.

There are two major problem themes identified in the current experience:
  1. Current GPA calculator alternatives are cumbersome, requiring users to manually input individual scores, grades and subject weights into an online table
  2. A significant amount of user time and concentration is required to input transcript grades into the calculator

Proposed Solution

The proposed solution is composed of two underlying capabilities:
  • An easy-to-use solution that minimises barriers for the user to access their GPA scores
  • A reliable solution that minimises human error and provides users with confidence in their score

Conclusion

The web app successfully takes manual inputs and returns GPA equivalents. Next steps include parsing Python PDF scraping outputs and converting them automatically into GPA equivalents.
